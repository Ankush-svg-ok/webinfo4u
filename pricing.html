Frequently Asked Questions

Are there any hidden fees?

No. What you see is what you pay. Transaction fees are listed clearly, and there are no setup fees, monthly minimums (on Startup), or cancellation penalties.

Can I switch plans anytime?

Yes. Upgrade or downgrade at any time. Changes take effect at the start of your next billing cycle with prorated adjustments.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept all major credit/debit cards, ACH transfers, and wire transfers for Enterprise plans.

Is there a free trial for Growth plan?

Yes! Growth plans come with a 14-day free trial. No credit card required to start.

Do you offer non-profit discounts?

Yes. Eligible non-profits receive 25% off Growth plans and waived platform fees. Contact our sales team for details.
