Our Journey

From Idea to Global Scale

2020

The Beginning

Founded by ex-Stripe and Goldman Sachs engineers who saw the gap in accessible financial APIs for SMBs.

2021

Seed Funding

Raised $8M seed round. Launched beta with core payment processing APIs and onboarded first 100 merchants.

2022

Series A & Product-Market Fit

$25M Series A led by a16z. Reached $1B in annualized payment volume. Team grew to 60.

2023

BaaS Launch

Launched Banking-as-a-Service. Added 2,000+ merchants. Expanded to 40 countries.

2024

Series B — $80M

Raised Series B at $1.2B valuation. 10B+ transactions processed. SOC 2 certification achieved.

2026

Global Expansion

10,000+ companies in 135 countries. $45B+ processed. 200+ employees across SF, London, Singapore.
